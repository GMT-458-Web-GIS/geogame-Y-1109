How to Play?

Welcome to INTERCOUNTRY! This is an exciting map-based game where your knowledge of countries and borders will be put to the test. Here’s how to play:

  • Start the game by clicking the "Start Game" button on the main screen.
  • You’ll be given a map showing the globe, with a timer counting down from 120 seconds.
  • On the right, you will see a list of three countries. Your task is to guess which country is located at the intersection of all three countries’ borders.
  • Click on one of the three options that appear below the map to make your guess.
  • If your guess is correct, you’ll earn 1 point, and the game will move on to the next round.
  • If you guess incorrectly, you will lose the game, and your score will be displayed.
  • The game lasts for 120 seconds. If you don’t answer all questions before time runs out, the game will end, and your score will be shown.

Your objective is to score as many points as possible within the 120 seconds. Good luck!

Remember: You must choose the country that shares borders with all three countries shown. If you need help, refer to the map!

We wish you all a great game!
