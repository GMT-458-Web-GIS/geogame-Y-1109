How to Play?

The INTERCOUNTRY game is a fun and educational map-based game. The game starts with a large display of the INTERCOUNTRY title flashing on the screen. Below this screen, there are two buttons: You can click the How to Play button to learn the game rules, or click the Start Game button to begin playing. Once the game starts, a 120-second countdown begins, and three countries will be displayed on the screen. These three countries will be the neighbors of the country you need to guess. The objective is to identify the correct country that shares borders with all three of these countries. You should write your answer in the answer box on the screen in uppercase letters. If your answer is correct, you earn 1 point, and a new set of three countries will be presented. In the game, you earn 1 point for every correct answer. However, if you give a wrong answer, you lose the game, and your score will be displayed on the screen. The game duration is 120 seconds, and if time runs out, a "Time's Up" message will be displayed. During this time, you will earn 1 point for each correct guess of a country combination. It is possible to score up to 40 points by correctly guessing all the country combinations within the 120 seconds. The game is still in development, and more features will be added over time to make it more enjoyable. We wish you all a great game!
